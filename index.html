Meal planning, simplified.

Bitez is the collaborative meal planning, recipe, and grocery management app for iOS. Plan your meals, create shopping lists, and share with your household, all in one place.

Download on the App Store

Features

Collaborative Meal Planning

Plan your weekly meals with your family or housemates in real-time. Everyone stays in sync.

Recipe Management

Keep all your favorite recipes in one place. Easily add ingredients to your grocery list.

Smart Grocery Lists

Automatically generated grocery lists from your meal plan. Check off items as you shop.
